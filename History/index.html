Troop 202: 2020

2020 was one of the most chaotic years not for just Troop 202, but for the entire world. Due to Covid-19, lockdowns were initiated and the troop couldn’t meet in-person. This led to new forms of meetings. These meetings were online and were conducted through Zoom. While Covid-19 did shut down many programs such as Ransburg Summer Camp, many outings from March-December with the exception of some (Photos shown include in-person outings), created many new experiences were made online. The online Troop 202 summer camp was a unique one, especially the new fangled Scoutmaster Questival. While you weren’t really in a camp like Ransburg, you still go to merit badge classes along with some others that are not even offered at Ransburg. People made box ovens. There were many online outings where you would attend zoom meetings and have a ton of fun!
